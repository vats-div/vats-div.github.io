Bio

I am currently a Senior Data Science Engineer at Sailthru. Earlier, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Rice University, where I worked with Prof. Richard Baraniuk. Before coming to Rice, I spent a year at the Institute for Mathematics and its Applications (IMA) and also worked with Prof. Robert Nowak. Before that, I received my PhD from Carnegie Mellon University in 2011 while working with Prof. José Moura. Even before that, I received my BS in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics from The University of Texas at Austin. My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and statistics.
